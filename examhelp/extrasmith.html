Smith Chart

 

 

A Smith chart is shown above and is a graphical tool used for calculating impedance along transmission lines and SWR values for transmission line to antenna matching.  The chart consists of circles of constant values of resistance, (resistance circles), and arcs of constant values of reactance, (reactance arcs).  The outside circle is labeled with a value for each reactance arc and is called the reactance axis.  The vertical line that splits the Smith chart is where each resistance circle is labeled with a value and is called the resistance axis.
