Amateur Radio Governance and Regulation

 

The FCC regulates and enforces the rules for the Amateur Radio Service in the United States.  Rules and regulations are contained in part 97 of the FCC rules.  Part 97 can be accessed in full at the FCC site:

 

http://www.gpo.gov/fdsys/pkg/CFR-2009-title47-vol5/pdf/CFR-2009-title47-vol5-part97.pdf [pdf]

 

or, there is a more easily searchable version at the ARRL web site:

 

http://www.arrl.org/part-97-amateur-radio
