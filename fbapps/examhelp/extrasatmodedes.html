Satellite Mode Designators

 

Satellite mode designators indicate the uplink and downlink frequencies used by the satellite. Mode designators are typically written as two letters like U/V, where the first letter indicates the uplink frequency and the second letter indicates the downlink frequencies. The table below contains the mode designators and their corresponding frequencies. For more information see the Wikipedia page on amateur satellite transmissions.

 

Designator Band Frequency
H 15m 21 MHz
A 10m 29 MHz
V 2m 145 MHz
U 70 cm 435 MHz
L 23cm 1.2 GHz
S 13cm 2.4 GHz
S2 9cm 3.4 GHz
C 5cm 5 GHz
X 3cm 10 GHz
K 1.2cm 24 GHz
R 6mm 47 GHz
