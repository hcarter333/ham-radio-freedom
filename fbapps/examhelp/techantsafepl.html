Antenna Safety and Power Lines

 

When erecting an amateur station antenna, it is important to avoid electrical power lines. Accidental contact between the antenna and power lines can cause electrocution and even death.

 

There is a minimum safe distance that an antenna should be installed away from power lines. The rule is that if the antenna falls, no part of it should be able to come within 10 feet of any power line.

 

Antennas should never be attached to utility poles because of the possibility of the antenna touching electrical power lines.
